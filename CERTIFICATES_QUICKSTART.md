# Certificate of Completion - Quick Start Guide

## What's New?

Students who complete courses (reach 100% progress) now automatically earn **Certificates of Completion**!

## How to Earn a Certificate

### Step 1: Register for a Course
- Browse available courses on the Courses page
- Click on a course to view details
- Click **"Register"** and fill in your information
- Submit to enroll in the course

### Step 2: Access Course Resources
The course has 4 types of resources:
1. **Notes** - Study materials
2. **PDF** - Reference documents
3. **YouTube Playlist** - Video lectures
4. **Guidance** - Course guidance

Click on each resource to access it (opens in new tab).
**Progress increases 25% for each resource accessed** (4 resources = 100% progress)

### Step 3: Complete the Course
- Access all 4 resources to reach 100% completion
- When you click the 4th resource:
  - A congratulatory message appears: 🎉 "You've earned a Certificate of Completion!"
  - Your certificate is automatically issued
  - A "Certified" badge appears on the course card in "My Courses"

### Step 4: View Your Certificate
- Go to **"Certificates"** in the left sidebar
- See all your earned certificates displayed with:
  - Course name
  - Unique certificate number (format: CERT-...)
  - Completion date
  - Download button

## Certificate Details

Each certificate includes:
- **Certificate Number** - Unique identifier (e.g., CERT-1704923847352-A3X9K2B)
- **Course Name** - Name of the completed course
- **Completion Date** - Date you completed the course
- **Student Name** - Your name as registered
- **Status** - Issued or Downloaded
- **Issue Date** - Date certificate was generated

## Tips

2. **Track Progress** - Use "Progress" page to monitor your overall learning
3. **Share Achievement** - Download and share your certificates
4. **Complete Quickly** - You can complete a course in just a few minutes by accessing all resources

## Troubleshooting

### Certificate Not Appearing?
- Ensure you've accessed all 4 resources (progress should be 100%)
- Refresh the page after accessing the 4th resource
- Check the "Certificates" page to see if it's there

### Progress Not Updating?
- Try refreshing the page
- Make sure you're clicking the resource links (they should open in a new tab)
- Check if you're still enrolled in the course

### Can't See Certificates Button?
- You must be logged in as a student (not admin)
- Log out and log in as a student user if needed
- Refresh the page
